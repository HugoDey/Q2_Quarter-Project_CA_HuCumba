What We Do

Initiatives

At ARF Veterinary Clinic, we are committed to implementing impactful initiatives that align with our mission to provide exceptional veterinary care and promote animal welfare in Davao. Our initiatives focus on various aspects of veterinary medicine, community engagement, and education.


Past Initiatives

  • 1. Adoption Drives: Objective: Facilitate the adoption of homeless animals into loving homes. Progress: Successful adoption events held in collaboration with local adoption agencies, resulting in numerous pets finding forever homes. Outcomes: Reduced stray animal population, increased community awareness of adoption benefits.


    • Ongoing Commitment

    Our commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of animals in Davao remains steadfast. We continue to explore new initiatives, partnerships, and collaborations that align with our mission and contribute to the well-being of the community.


    Involvements

    ARF Veterinary Clinic is grateful for the support, partnerships, and affiliations that strengthen our ability to provide exceptional veterinary care and promote animal welfare in Davao. Our network of partners, supporters, and affiliated organizations plays a crucial role in achieving our mission.


    Activities and Participation

    At ARF Veterinary Clinic, our diverse range of activities reflects our commitment to providing comprehensive veterinary care, promoting responsible pet ownership, and actively engaging with the community in Davao. These activities aim to address the varied needs of our furry companions and enhance the overall well-being of our community
